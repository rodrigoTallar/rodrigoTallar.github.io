I’m currently learning a lot of difficult and mostly useless things at the university. All for the sake of obtaining a piece of paper called diploma. Despite my enthusiasm, I must confess that I'm not particularly skilled at them, but the fun is in the experience. I built the PC I use for gaming, and I'm quite proud of it.
